Lab 1

Network Measures

Pick one real network from ICON. Using Python + NetworkX in Google Colab, compute and visualize:

  • Degree distribution, and average & max degree
  • Shortest path-length distribution, avg path length, diameter
  • Clustering-coefficient distribution, average clustering coefficient

You may consult the NetworkX docs; you are encouraged to ask your favorite LLM to help generate/clean up code (cite the prompt you used).

What to produce

  1. Notebook (.ipynb) with runnable cells and comments.
  2. Plots (PNG in the repo/drive and displayed in the notebook):
    • Degree histogram (linear and loglog)
    • Path-length histogram (per giant component; state how you handled disconnected nodes)
    • Clustering-coefficient histogram
  3. One-page write-up (in the notebook or a short PDF section):
    • Dataset description (source, nodes/edges, your preprocessing)
    • Numeric summary: average & max degree, avg path length, diameter, average clustering coefficient
    • A few sentences interpreting each plot (not just restating numbers)

Detailed information and requirements please refer to Lab1.pdf.
